My Favourite Movie: Hairspray (2007)

Hairspray is a movie about an overnight dancing sensation, Tracy, on the "Corny Collins" show. Tracy's main goal is to bring racial integration to the foreground on television. I appreciate that this movie discusses hard to deal with topics and gives an overview of life and equality gaps in the 1960s. The cast picks and acting are wonderful. As well, the music is another large comfort component of this movie.

Cast List:

John Travolta, Queen Latifah, Nikki Blonsky, Michelle Pfeiffer, Christopher Walken, Amanda Bynes, Zac Efron, Elijah Kelley, James Marsden, Brittany Snow

Funniest Line From The Movie:

"Detroit sound? What's that? The cries of people being mugged?"- Velma Von Tussle (Michelle Pfeiffer)
